OUR NEW CURRICULUM

Elevating Design through Project-Based Learning

By the Editorial Team

April 17, 2024

Dive into the elegance of our revamped educational experience, where learning transcends traditional bounds through project-based creativity. Forget mundane coding challenges; this curriculum invites you to construct your design portfolio piece by piece, ensuring mastery in each stitch of your digital fabric.

While the essence of our certifications remains untouched, each adorned with five pivotal projects, the ancillary exercises are reimagined to dress you for success in the technology atelier. Imagine the burst of clarity that comes with a revolutionary idea—like unveiling the next trend in haute couture.

This epiphany didn't strike under the glare of runway lights but emerged during a serene moment of reflection. The future of learning here is an artful collection of projects, each crafted to challenge and inspire, with just enough guidance to propel you forward without stifling creativity. Hints linger in the wings, should you need a whisper of inspiration.

The curriculum is now a runway of projects

Gone are the verbose explanations and barrage of tests. Now, you'll encounter one elegant challenge at a time, each step building towards a masterpiece. As you glide through these projects, you’ll stitch your understanding of coding fundamentals into a gown of expertise. The transition between lessons and creations is seamless, woven together into a single, coherent learning tapestry.

Transforming paragraphs of dry instructions into crisp, clear test descriptions, each project invites you to code with style, continually crafting as you learn. Instead of disjointed challenges, envision yourself in a studio, fabric in hand, crafting one garment after another, effortlessly flowing into the state of creation that artists and designers cherish.

This new approach not only smooths the learning experience but enlivens it, ensuring that every learner feels the thrill of progress from the very first stitch.

A Brief History

Of the Curriculum

  • 2014 - The Debut

    Launched with an ensemble of 15 resources, our curriculum included prestigious workshops like Harvard's CS50 and Stanford's Database Class.

  • 2015 - The Sophomore Collection

    We introduced interactive algorithm challenges to our lineup.

  • 2015 - Expanding the Wardrobe

    Our curriculum grew richer with bespoke HTML+CSS challenges, stepping beyond the foundational courses offered by others.

  • 2016 - The Trilogy

    We broadened our scope to include three comprehensive certifications—Front End, Back End, and Data Visualization—each demanding ten unique creations.

  • 2017 - Adding Depth

    We enriched our offerings with sophisticated backend and data visualization challenges.

  • 2018 - The Revolution

    A complete overhaul introduced six new certifications, marking our most significant evolution in curriculum design.

Our learners, akin to haute couture patrons, will discover a curriculum that masterfully blends classic techniques with modern flair, empowering them to craft their technological journey.
